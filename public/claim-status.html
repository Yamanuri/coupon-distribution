About Our Coupon Distribution System

The Coupon Distribution System is designed to help users discover and claim valuable discounts from various merchants. Our system ensures fair distribution by implementing a cooldown period between claims.

This system was developed as part of an internship assignment to demonstrate proficiency in web development and user experience design.

Key features include:

  • Browse available coupons from different categories
  • Claim coupons with a simple form
  • Check eligibility status in real-time
  • View countdown timer for next eligible claim
